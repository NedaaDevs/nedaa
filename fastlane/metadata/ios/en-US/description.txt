Nedaa is a free, open-source, privacy-first prayer-times and Islamic-companion app. No data collection. No ads. No accounts. No paywalls. No premium tier.

Nedaa (Arabic: نداء, "the call" — a reference to the Adhan) is built around accurate prayer times, real OS-level Fajr alarms, daily Athkar, Qibla, Hijri tools, an Umrah guide, a Qada (fasting) tracker, and a Quran reader in active development.

ACCURATE PRAYER TIMES — ANYWHERE
• 22 calculation methods (Muslim World League, ISNA, Umm al-Qura, Karachi, Egyptian, Diyanet, JAKIM, Kemenag, Singapore MUIS, and more)
• Both Hanafi and standard Asr juristic schools
• High-latitude adjustment
• Works offline after first sync — no internet needed afterwards
• Provider-abstracted at the API layer, currently powered by the Aladhan API

A PRAYER ALARM THAT ACTUALLY WAKES YOU
• Real OS-level alarms — not notifications
• Apple AlarmKit on iOS 26+ (alarm feature requires iOS 26)
• Three first-class alarm types: Fajr, Jummah, and custom
• Bypasses Do Not Disturb and silent mode
• Optional wake-up challenges (tap or math)
• Custom Athan sound per alarm type

DAILY ATHKAR
• Morning and evening remembrances from the Hisn al-Muslim dataset (Sa'id ibn Ali ibn Wahaf al-Qahtani)
• Multiple reciter audio voices
• Focus-mode reader
• Custom user-added athkar
• Gentle, non-gamified habit tracking with pause/resume

UMRAH GUIDE
• Step-by-step guide for every ritual
• Full Prophetic dua at Safa and Marwa
• Everything you need offline, in one place

QADA FASTING TRACKER
• Counts down missed Ramadan fasts
• Ramadan-anchored reminder for the canonical "make up before next Ramadan" deadline
• Privacy-mode hide

WIDGETS
• Home Screen widgets for all prayers or just the next one
• Lock Screen widgets with countdown timers
• Ramadan-ready: Suhoor and Iftar widgets

PRIVACY-FIRST BY DESIGN
• Low-accuracy location, used only to compute prayer times — stays on device
• No advertising ID, no install graph, no contacts access, no profile across devices
• Open source — audit how your data is handled at github.com/NedaaDevs/nedaa
• Source transparency: Tanzil Project (Quran text, CC-BY 3.0), Hisn al-Muslim (athkar), Aladhan (prayer times) — all credited in the app

LANGUAGES
• English, Arabic, Malay, Urdu — RTL-first design
